2D-Kinematics Simulator

How to Use ▼

This simulator models 2D projectile motion using basic physics equations.

  • Adjust velocity and angle to change trajectory
  • Change gravity or use planet presets
  • Adjust mass (affects energy calculations)
  • Press Start to launch
  • Use Pause or Reset

Watch height, range, and energy evolve in real time.

Model Note: Ideal projectile motion (no air resistance, no energy loss)
